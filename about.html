Our Heritage

Where Modern Luxury Meets Historical Grandeur

Founded in the roaring twenties as a private retreat for the world's elite, Paradise Heights Hotel has remained a beacon of sophistication for a century. What began as a modest hilltop lodge has evolved into a global icon of luxury hospitality, preserving the architectural soul of its heritage while embracing the pinnacle of modern comfort.

Every corridor tells a story, every suite whispers a secret of the past. Our commitment to preserving this legacy is matched only by our dedication to providing a sanctuary where the quiet luxury of the present meets the prestigious echoes of history.
